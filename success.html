Welcome to Raja Mantri Chor Sipahi

About the Game

Raja Mantri Chor Sipahi is a classic Indian detective game that combines strategy, deception, and fun. In this game, players are secretly assigned roles of Raja (King), Mantri (Minister), Chor (Thief), and Sipahi (Police), and must use their wit to win.

The Mantri must try to catch the Chor while other players attempt to fulfill their respective roles. The game tests your ability to read people, make strategic decisions, and think on your feet!

Game Rules & Scoring

  • Raja (King) scores 1000 points by default
  • Mantri (Minister) scores 800 points if they catch the Chor
  • Sipahi (Police) scores 400 points
  • Chor (Thief) scores 0 points if caught
  • If Mantri fails to catch Chor:
    • Mantri's score becomes 0
    • Chor's score becomes 800
